Key Insights

Portuguese Student Data Insights
We found that there is a pretty even distribution amongst students who live in an urban environment and have internet no matter whether they do extracurriculars.
Economics students in the US Data Insights
If a student got above a 600 on the Math section of the SAT then they almost absolutely took math classes. We observed that there was actually a higher distribution of students who did better than a 600 on the SAT if that student was not an Econ Major. We could also observe that also no Social Science students did better than a 700 on the Math aspect of the SAT.
International Student Data Insights
If the students had many absences, they did not typically have high grades regardless of gender. We also observed an interesting phenomenon that for males with bad grades, they had the highest amount of parental satisfaction in the school whereas the percentage of women whose parents were satisfied was higher if they had high grades.
